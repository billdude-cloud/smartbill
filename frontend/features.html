5 Flexible Split Methods

Equal Split

Split the bill equally among all group members. Perfect for when everyone shares equally.

By Percentage

Assign different percentages to each member. Great for income-based splits.

Custom Amounts

Enter specific dollar amounts for each person. Total must equal the bill amount.

Item-Based Split

Assign each item to specific members. Perfect for restaurants where people order different things.

Unequal Shares

Set different share counts (e.g., 2x for someone who ate more). Value per share is calculated automatically.
