The Story Behind SmartBill

As a developer, I've always been passionate about creating tools that make everyday life easier. The idea for SmartBill came from a common frustration - every time I went out with friends or shared expenses with roommates, we'd end up with complicated calculations, IOUs, and sometimes awkward conversations about money.

I wanted to build something that would eliminate that friction completely. A tool that would automatically calculate who owes what, track payments, and keep everyone informed - all while being simple and intuitive to use.

What started as a side project quickly evolved into a full-stack application with real-time payment tracking, multiple split methods, group management, and a clean, responsive interface. Today, SmartBill helps groups of all sizes manage their shared expenses without the headache.

Development Journey
