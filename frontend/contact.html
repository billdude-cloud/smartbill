Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to messages?

I typically respond within 24-48 hours during weekdays. For urgent issues, you can reach me on WhatsApp or Telegram for faster response.

Can I report a bug?

Absolutely! Please use the contact form and include detailed steps to reproduce the bug, what browser you're using, and any error messages you see. Screenshots are very helpful!

Do you offer support for businesses?

Yes! I'm open to discussing custom solutions for businesses, teams, or organizations. Please reach out with your requirements via email or WhatsApp.

Is SmartBill open source?

Currently, SmartBill is a private project. However, I'm open to collaboration opportunities. Contact me if you're interested in contributing.
